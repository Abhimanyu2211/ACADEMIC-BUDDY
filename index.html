About Academic Buddy

We're revolutionizing academic support by creating a community where students help students. Our platform makes it easy to find trustworthy peer tutors, access quality study materials, and get guidance from students who've excelled in your courses.

Founded by students for students, we understand the challenges you face and have built solutions that actually work for your academic needs.

Our Services

CAEG/CAMD Sheet Help

Get assistance with your engineering design sheets from peers who've aced these courses.

Assignment Assistance

Stuck on an assignment? Connect with tutors who can guide you without doing the work for you.

E-Notes & Study Materials

Access curated digital notes, past papers, textbooks, and comprehensive study guides shared by top students.

Topic-wise Tutoring

Struggling with specific concepts? Get 1-on-1 help exactly where you need it.

Academic Counseling

Guidance on course selection, study techniques, and overcoming academic challenges.

Career Guidance

Connect with seniors who can share insights about career paths and opportunities.
